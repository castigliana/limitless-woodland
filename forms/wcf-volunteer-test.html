World Culture Festival (WCF) is a movement for creating a more harmonious and compassionate world by celebrating humanity’s rich cultural diversity and the underlying spirit that connects us all.

The Global Leadership Forum (GLF) will take place in conjunction with the festival. This is a curated and inclusive platform to engage in collaborative activities to address global issues in four crucial areas that stand out as urgent.
